INSTRUCTIONS FOR RECOMB PROCEEDINGS

This web page contains the guidelines and pertinent information for the final submission of papers for the First Annual International Conference on Computational Molecular Biology (RECOMB'97).

As indicated below, it is particularly important that you leave sufficient space in the lower left corner of the first column on the first page for ACM to fill in the copyright information. There is a 10-page limit on your paper including everything. You may purchase up to 2 extra pages at $200 per page. The check should be made out to RECOMB'97.

Please submit a camera-ready copy produced on a high-resolution out-put device of your manuscript.

The camera-ready format for 8 1/2" x 11" paper is:

2 column with no page numbers:

Font: English Times or Times Roman , minimum 9 pt. Column width: 3.33" 2 column gutter: .33" Left and Right Margin: from edge .75" Top margin: .75" Bottom margin: 1.00" Copyright space on 1st page: lower left column 1"

A LaTeX style file for the these proceedings can be retreived here.

Checklist

  • The ACM copyright form must be signed and returned with your paper. ACM will not publish any paper that is not accompanied by a signed copyright form. In those cases where there is more than one author per paper, the first-named (assumed senior) author only must sign.
  • Sign the first author's name and page number by pencil on the back of each page. Do not use staples, paper clips, or anything that will bend, fold, punch holes through or leave marks on the paper.
  • Your paper and form MUST arrive before November 10 in order to be included in the proceedings.
  • Please federal express or mail your paper and signed copyright form to Mike Waterman:
