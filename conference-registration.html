RECOMB 97 REGISTRATION INFORMATION

The registration fees for RECOMB'97 are listed below. To qualify for the early registration fee, your registration application must be postmarked by Friday, December 12. Refund requests will be honored until January 4. The non-student registration fee includes the Sunday night reception, the Tuesday night banquet, coffee breaks and lunches, and a copy of the proceedings. The student fee includes all of the above except the banquet.
